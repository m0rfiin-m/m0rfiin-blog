This timeline documents the development of my digital leadership portfolio across my Master's in Entertainment Business at Full Sail University. It covers both the academic work that informed my brand and the practical steps taken to build a public-facing portfolio.

**September 2025 - Program Launch**
- Enrolled in the Master's in Entertainment Business at Full Sail University
- Established core program goals: move from production and operations into the strategic business side of entertainment
- Identified target industries: esports, gaming, community operations, entertainment business
- Began building LinkedIn presence as a professional platform

**October 2025 - Brand Identity Research**
- Completed coursework on brand positioning and personal brand development
- Identified unique value proposition: business-side entertainment professional with hands-on community management experience
- Began LinkedIn Learning certifications in marketing and business analysis to strengthen credentials

**November 2025 - Credential Building**
- Completed 20+ LinkedIn Learning certifications across digital marketing, business analysis, project management, and data management

- Drafted initial personal brand statement

## Phase 2: Strategy Development (December 2025 - February 2026)

**December 2025 - Content Planning**
- Built a content calendar in Notion to plan consistent digital output

**January 2026 - Tempest eSports Concept Begins**

**February 2026 - Deep Research Phase**
